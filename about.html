About Wellnest

Wellnest is your personal companion for emotional wellness and mental clarity. Our platform connects you with professional counsellors, making it easy to schedule sessions and prioritize your mental health.

Our Mission

To make mental health care accessible, confidential, and stigma-free through seamless digital experiences.

Why Wellnest?

  • Verified professional counsellors
  • Simple, secure session booking
  • User-friendly and intuitive interface
  • Confidential and encrypted data handling
