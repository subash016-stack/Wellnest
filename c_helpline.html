Child Helpline Access

Purpose of Child Helplines

Child helplines are dedicated 24/7 services that provide immediate support, guidance, and intervention for children facing danger, abuse, or distress. They are a vital tool for child protection.

Key Features

  • Free and confidential support over phone or chat.
  • Trained counselors to assist children and guide them to safety.
  • Direct coordination with child protection agencies and authorities.
  • Follow-up and rehabilitation services when needed.

India's Child Helpline

1098 is India's national 24-hour helpline for children in need of care and protection. It is toll-free and accessible from anywhere in the country.
