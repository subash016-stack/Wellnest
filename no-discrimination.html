No Discrimination in Schools

Equal Treatment for All Students

Every child has the right to be treated equally in educational institutions regardless of gender, religion, caste, ethnicity, disability, or socioeconomic background.

Inclusive Policies

Schools are legally required to implement inclusive admission and disciplinary policies, ensuring fair opportunities for learning and participation without any bias.

Awareness and Training

Teachers and school staff should receive training to recognize and prevent discriminatory practices and foster a respectful and supportive environment.

Right to Redress

Students and parents have the right to report discrimination and seek corrective measures through school authorities or legal channels.
