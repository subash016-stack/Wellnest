Right to Free and Compulsory Education

Overview

Every child has the right to receive free and compulsory education. This ensures that no child is denied access to basic education due to financial constraints or social barriers.

Age Coverage

This right typically applies to children aged 6 to 14, ensuring they complete elementary education without fees or hidden charges.

Responsibilities

Governments and educational institutions are mandated to provide the necessary infrastructure, teachers, and learning materials to support free education.

Inclusion and Equality

The law ensures non-discrimination in schools and promotes inclusion of children from all backgrounds, including marginalized and differently-abled children.
