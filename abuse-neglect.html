Protection from Abuse and Neglect

Understanding the Right

Every child has the right to live in a safe and secure environment, free from all forms of abuse and neglect. This includes physical, emotional, and sexual harm as well as deprivation of basic needs like food, shelter, and care.

Key Safeguards

  • Strict laws and policies against child abuse and domestic violence.
  • Training for teachers and caregivers to detect and report abuse early.
  • Child-friendly complaint mechanisms in schools and institutions.
  • Support centers and shelters for children facing abuse or abandonment.

What to Do

If a child is facing abuse or neglect, they or a trusted adult should immediately contact a child helpline or report the issue to local authorities. Protection laws ensure confidentiality and swift action.
